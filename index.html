About Me
Collins Nji

4+Years Exp.
50+Sessions
3Specialties

I'm a photographer based in the Raleigh–Durham–Chapel Hill area with a passion for storytelling through light, composition, and moments. Whether it's the raw power of a machine, the energy of a live event, or the quiet depth of a portrait — every image I create is crafted with intention.

With 4+ years of experience and 50+ sessions under my belt, I blend technical precision with a genuine artistic eye to deliver photographs that are both emotionally resonant and timeless.
